PLACEHOLDER FOR PROFILE PHOTO

To add your profile photo:
1. Choose a professional headshot (square ratio recommended)
2. Optimize for web (recommended: 500x500px, under 200KB)
3. Save as "profile-photo.jpg"
4. Place in this /public directory

Supported formats:
- JPG/JPEG (recommended)
- PNG
- WebP (best compression)

Photo tips:
- Use good lighting
- Professional attire
- Neutral background
- Smile naturally
- High resolution (at least 500x500px)
